Five Easy Payments


We made FiveEasyPayments at The Flatiron School for one of our final projects. This app uses the Venmo API and provides users with a deep view of their Venmo finances. It includes a D3 visualization and a "Venmo Credit Score" which we generate to show you how well you've paid off your friends.

FiveEasyPayments is created by Nate Kratchman, Benjamin Gross and myself.
